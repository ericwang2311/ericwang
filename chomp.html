Chomp

Eric Wang
AP Computer Science, 2017

For my highschool AP Computer Science class, I was tasked with creating the a.i. for the game "Chomp". In Chomp, the player and a.i. take turns "chomping" off pieces of a candy bar until there is a single piece left. The person that takes the last piece of the candy bar (denoted by the X) loses the game. While the game may look simple, my task in this project was to make sure that the a.i. could NEVER lose to the human player which definitely took a fair share of time.
