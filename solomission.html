Solo Mission

Eric Wang
Personal Project, 2018

During the summer of 2018, I took interest in app development and followed a video series on how to make a basic phone game titled "Solo Mission". The video series was created by Youtuber Matt Heaney and acts as a coding tutorial on how to develop a game from start to finish in XCode. The game itself is very similar to "Space Invaders" with its concept of spaceships, lasers, and seemingly endless enemy spaceships. While I had made decent progress into the series, I was soon caught up in other things and the personal project was left on hold.
